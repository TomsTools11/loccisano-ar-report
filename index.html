How Your Bids Actually Work

A 60-second walkthrough of base bid, source multipliers, and why Cost Per Click (CPC) varies across the campaign
The short version
Your $7 isn't a flat price — it's a starting point you use to compete in dozens of separate auctions.
Each traffic source (search, email, referral, etc.) is its own marketplace. We give you one base bid and tune source-by-source with multipliers. The market decides the final clearing price for every click. Sometimes that's $5; sometimes it's $13.

The Formula

Base Bid×Source Multiplier=Effective Bid
Multiplier of 100% means "bid your base." 125% means "bid 25% more on this source." 90% means "bid 10% less."
